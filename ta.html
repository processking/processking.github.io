    Product Statement

    With so many apps out there trying to solve food waste the food waste problem still remains.

    111 billion pounds of good food is thrown into landfills every year.

    TakeAway is a convenience system that would be integrated into grocery chains that waste billions of pounds of food annually. People would pay a small monthly subscription fee to gain access to one food box per a day. This will be mutually beneficial to reducing food waste in our landfills and reducing food insecurity in our communities.
