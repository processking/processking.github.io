    Branding
    + Identity

    We are a local company focused on delivering great granola in small batches. We need an rebrand to represent ourselves in a saturated market. We are fun company, hence our great name. We want to retain our fun feel while presenting a healthy and delicous product.

    Outline

    Our main objective is to create graphic identity for LNYF that can be used for years to come. A mark that is reused through each year to build brand recongization. This mark must be recognizable, have fluid application properties, scale to any size, and represent the organization. We will then take this identity and apply it to applications or user touchpoints. Lasty we will redesign the web experience to remain cohessive with our brand identity.
