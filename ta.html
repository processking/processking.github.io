    Product Statement

    TakeAway is a convenience system that would be integrated into grocery chains. Grocery chains are responsible for billions of pounds of food waste annually. Consumers would subscribe to gain access to one food box per a day. This would be mutually beneficial to reducing food waste in our landfills and reducing food insecurity in our communities.
