m42

Fine Wine

m42 is the pinnacle of human perfection. Only premium grapes go into our custom limited batches. m42 values the finer things in life and many argue that m42 is the meaning of life itself. We invite you to taste what the universe has been crafting for over 14 billion years.

m42 is based on Messier 42, known as the Orion Nebula. The number 42 has associations with the meaning of life and the ultimate question, a notion derived from the tale of the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.
