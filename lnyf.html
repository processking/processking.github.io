    Lunar New Year Festival 2018. Having a lunar new year festival is a long standing tradition in cities across the world. I wanted to retain some of the tradition aspects of the festival but also wanted to generate higher attendence and get more young adults interested in the event. I looked to rebrand the festival with modern appeal to attract a different audience.

    Outline

    The main objective is to create a graphic identity for LNYF that can be used for years to come. A mark that is reused through each year to build brand recongization. This mark must be recognizable, have fluid application properties, scale to any size, and represent the organization. Then take this identity and apply it to applications or user touchpoints. Lasty redesign the web experience to remain cohessive with the brand identity.
